WATER DISPENSER RENTAL AGREEMENT

This agreement is between Waterboy Delivery ("Company") and the customer ("Renter").

1. RENTAL TERM: The Renter agrees to rent the selected water dispenser for the duration chosen above, beginning on the delivery date.

2. MONTHLY PAYMENT: The Renter agrees to pay the monthly rental fee as specified. Payment will be charged automatically on the same date each month.

3. SECURITY DEPOSIT: A refundable security deposit of $50.00 will be charged at the start of the rental. This deposit is returned in full upon return of the dispenser in good working condition.

4. CARE & MAINTENANCE: The Renter agrees to use the dispenser for its intended purpose only. Normal wear and tear is expected. The Renter is responsible for any damage beyond normal use.

5. MAINTENANCE & REPAIRS: Waterboy Delivery will provide free maintenance and repairs for any issues not caused by misuse. Contact us to schedule a service visit.

6. EARLY TERMINATION: The Renter may end the rental early by contacting Waterboy Delivery. An early termination fee of one month's rental rate will apply if cancelled before the agreed term ends.

7. RETURN: At the end of the rental term, the Renter may: (a) renew the rental, (b) purchase the dispenser at a discounted price, or (c) schedule a free pickup. The dispenser must be returned in working condition.

8. PURCHASE OPTION: At any time during the rental, the Renter may purchase the dispenser. The purchase price will be reduced by 50% of all rental payments made to date.

9. DELIVERY & SETUP: Initial delivery and setup is FREE. Waterboy Delivery will install the dispenser and ensure it is working properly.

10. LIABILITY: Waterboy Delivery is not liable for any injury or damage resulting from improper use of the dispenser.
